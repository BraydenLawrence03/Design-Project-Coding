Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Celiac Disease


Celiac is an autoimmune disease that means you can't consume any form of wheat or gluten. You aren't able to properly digest any form of wheat and gluten and if consumed results in sickness, extreme pain and discomfort or passing out.


Is Gluten Free Hard?


Gluten free is not as hard as it used to be. Thanks to modern food research, most foods have gluten free alternatives. You can find gluten free alterantives at almost any store and most foods are trying to refrain from using gluten and wheat as it's not the most healthy.


Is It Hard Going To Restaurants Being Gluten Free?

You would be suprised how easy it is to be gluten free at restaurants nowadays. Most waiters will know what it is and to prevent cross contamination, they can cook the gluten free food seperate from the wheat and gluten products. Also, most restaurants have gluten free menus or gluten free options.
