Small Tips For A Gluten Free Diet

Always check the ingredients for everything, even if you don't think there is any gluten or wheat in it.

When going to a restaurant, don't be afraid to ask the waiter if the food is gluten free.

Don't be too distraut about being celiac, most foods nowadays have gluten free alternatives.

Be careful of cross contamination at restaurants. Most foods are cooked with the same oil and or deep fried with the same oil as wheat foods.
